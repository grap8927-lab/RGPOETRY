Time & Journey Poems

Footsteps of Dawn

Each sunrise marks another path beneath my feet...

Clock Without Hands

Time drifts unmeasured when love carries me forward...

Bridge of Years

I cross memories like stepping stones into tomorrow...

Turning Seasons

Leaves fall, snow melts, yet I remain walking on...

Wanderer’s Path

My heart carries maps unseen, guiding every step...

Sands of Memory

Grains slip between fingers, yet some moments remain...

Shadow of Tomorrow

Even in today’s light, tomorrow casts its outline...

Miles Behind, Miles Ahead

The road stretches in both directions, yet I keep moving...

Eternal Road

No ending waits, only bends unseen beyond the horizon...

Keeper of Time

Somewhere, someone holds the key to every moment lived...
